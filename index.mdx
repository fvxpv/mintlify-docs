## Welcome to Covet

**Covet** is a **free & multi-purpose** premium Discord bot designed to make your server safer, cleaner, and easier to manage. Used by **thousands of members** across multiple servers, Covet provides comprehensive moderation, utility, fun, and engagement features to enhance your Discord community.
